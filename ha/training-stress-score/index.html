Training Stress Score (TSS): Cikakken Jagora don Masu Gudu

An buga: Janairu 27, 2025 | An sabunta: Janairu 29, 2025

Amsa Mai Sauri

Training Stress Score (TSS) wani ma'auni ne da ke auna tsananin da tsawon lokacin horo zuwa lamba guda ɗaya da ke wakiltar nauyin horo. Don gudu (sTSS), ana lissafa shi azaman: Lokaci (auwoyi) × Intensity Factor³ × 100, inda Intensity Factor = Gudun Horo / Mahimmin Gudun Gudu (CRS).

Mahimman Bayanai:

  • Sa'a 1 na horo a matakin ƙarfi (threshold) = 100 TSS
  • Yana bin diddigin lafiyar jiki (CTL), gajiya (ATL), da tsari (TSB)
  • Manufofin mako-mako: 300-500 TSS (masu nishaɗi) zuwa 800-1200+ TSS (ƙwararru)
  • Yana taimakawa hana yawan horo yayin tabbatar da isasshen kuzarin horo

Training Stress Score (TSS) yana auna nauyin horo na ɗaiɗaikun horo da tarin damuwar horo akan lokaci. Asali an haɓaka shi don tseren keke, an daidaita TSS don gudu (sTSS) don taimakawa 'yan wasa daidaita tsananin horo, sarrafa gajiya, da haɓaka aiki. Fahimtar TSS yana ba da damar yanke shawarar horo bisa bayanai wanda ke hana yawan horo yayin tabbatar da isasshen kuzari don daidaitawa.

Wannan cikakken jagorar yana bayyana menene TSS, yadda ake lissafa shi don gudu, yadda ake amfani da ma'aunin TSS kamar CTL (lafiyar jiki), ATL (gajiya), da TSB (tsari) don sarrafa horon ku, da yadda ake haɗa TSS cikin matakan horo daban-daban. Ko kuna horo don 5K ko marathon, TSS yana ba da amsa na haƙiƙa wanda kimantawa na zahiri ba zai iya daidaitawa ba.

Menene Training Stress Score (TSS)?

Training Stress Score lamba ce guda ɗaya da ke wakiltar jimlar nauyin horo na motsa jiki, la'akari da duka tsawon lokaci da tsanani. Ba kamar bin diddigin mil ko lokaci kawai ba, TSS yana auna tsanani yadda ya kamata: mintuna 60 na gudu mai tsanani (tempo) yana haifar da damuwar horo fiye da mintuna 60 na gudu mai sauƙi.

Mahimman Ra'ayoyi a Bayan TSS

  • Auna na Haƙiƙa: TSS yana ba da daidaitaccen ma'auni don kwatanta horo daban-daban—intervals vs tempo vs dogon gudu
  • Auna Tsanani: Horo mai tsananin gaske yana haifar da damuwa fiye da yadda tsawon lokaci kaɗai zai nuna
  • Bin Diddigin Tari: Ana iya taƙaice TSS kowace rana, mako-mako, da kuma kan hanyoyin horo don bin diddigin jimlar nauyin horo

sTSS: TSS don Gudu

Yayin da aka asalin haɓaka TSS ta amfani da bayanan ƙarfin keke, sTSS (running Training Stress Score) yana daidaita manufar don gudu ta amfani da sauri maimakon ƙarfi. Tsarin asali na dabarar ya kasance iri ɗaya, amma sTSS yana amfani da Mahimmin Gudun Gudu (CRS) ko saurin matakin ƙarfi a matsayin tushe.

Me yasa CRS don sTSS?

CRS yana wakiltar saurin ku mai dorewa na aerobic-anaerobic—a zahiri "saurin matakin ƙarfi na aiki." Amfani da CRS azaman ma'auni yana tabbatar da:

  • Lissafin tsanani na musamman ga jikin ku
  • Daidaitaccen kwatance tsakanin matakan gudu daban-daban

Yadda ake Lissafa sTSS

Dabarar sTSS tana la'akari da duka tsawon lokacin horo da tsanani dangane da matakin ƙarfin ku:

sTSS = (Tsawon lokaci a cikin daƙiƙa × IF³ × 100) / 3600

Inda:
IF = Intensity Factor (saurin horo / saurin CRS)
Tsawon lokaci = Jimlar lokacin horo a cikin daƙiƙa

Misalin Lissafi Mataki-mataki

Horo: Mintuna 60 na gudu mai tsanani a saurin 4:00/km
CRS ɗin ku: 4:20/km (270 m/min ko 4.5 m/s)

Mataki 1: Canza saurin zuwa m/s

  • Saurin horo: 4:00/km = 4.17 m/s
  • Saurin CRS: 4:20/km = 3.85 m/s

Mataki 2: Lissafa Intensity Factor (IF)

IF = Saurin horo / Saurin CRS
IF = 4.17 / 3.85 = 1.08

Mataki 3: Lissafa sTSS

Tsawon lokaci = Mintuna 60 = 3,600 daƙiƙa
sTSS = (3,600 × 1.08² × 100) / 3,600
sTSS = (3,600 × 1.166 × 100) / 3,600
sTSS = 116.6 ≈ 117

Wannan mintuna 60 na gudu mai tsanani a 108% na CRS yana haifar da 117 sTSS—dan kadan fiye da damuwa fiye da sa'a ɗaya a matakin ƙarfi zai samar.

Fahimtar Intensity Factor (IF)

Intensity Factor shine rabo na saurin horon ku zuwa saurin matakin ƙarfin ku.

Ma'aunin CTL, ATL, da TSB

TSS don ɗaiɗaikun horo yana da amfani, amma ainihin ƙarfin yana zuwa ne daga bin diddigin ma'aunin tari akan lokaci. Ma'auni uku da aka samo suna sarrafa dogon lokacin nauyin horo:

Chronic Training Load (CTL): Lafiyar Jiki

CTL shine dogon lokacin nauyin horon ku. CTL yana wakiltar lafiyar jikin ku—ƙarfin ku na ɗaukar damuwar horo.

Acute Training Load (ATL): Gajiya

ATL shine gajeren lokacin nauyin horon ku. ATL yana wakiltar gajiyar ku—tasirin horon kwanan nan.

Training Stress Balance (TSB): Tsari

TSB shine bambanci tsakanin lafiyar jiki da gajiya. Yana hasashen tsarin ku—shirye-shiryen yin aiki ko horo mai tsanani.

TSB = CTL - ATL
