Tushen Kimiyya

Run Analytics an gina shi akan shekaru da yawa na binciken kimiyyar wasanni da aka yi bita:

Critical Run Speed (CRS)

Dangane da binciken Wakayoshi et al. (1992-1993) a Jami'ar Osaka. CRS tana wakiltar matsakaicin saurin gudu na ka'idar da za a iya dorewa ba tare da gajiya ba, daidai da kofofin lactate.

Mabuɗin Bincike: Wakayoshi K, et al. "Determination and validity of critical velocity as an index of running performance." European Journal of Applied Physiology, 1992.

Training Stress Score (TSS)

An daidaita shi daga hanyar TSS ta keke na Dr. Andrew Coggan don gudu. Yana ƙididdige nauyin horo ta hanyar haɗa tsananin (dangane da CRS) da tsawon lokaci.

Mabuɗin Bincike: Coggan AR, Allen H. "Training and Racing with a Power Meter." VeloPress, 2010. An daidaita don gudu ta Run Analytics ta amfani da CRS azaman kofa.

Performance Management Chart (PMC)

Matakan don Chronic Training Load (CTL), Acute Training Load (ATL) da Training Stress Balance (TSB). Yana bin diddigin dacewa, gajiya da tsari akan lokaci.

Aiwatarwa: Matsakaicin motsi mai nauyi na kwanaki 42 don CTL, kwanaki 7 don ATL. TSB = CTL - ATL.

Ingancin Gudu & Matakan Takawa

Matakan ingancin gudu waɗanda ke haɗa lokaci da kirgen matakai. Masu gudu masu kwarewa da kociyoyi a duniya suna amfani da su don bin diddigin ci gaban fasaha.

Matsakaicin Matakai: Ingancin Gudu = Lokaci + Matakai. Ƙananan maki suna nuna kyakkyawan inganci. An haɗa da Distance Per Stride (DPS) da Stride Rate (SR).
