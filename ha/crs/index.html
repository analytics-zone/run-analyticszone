Fahimtar Critical Run Speed (CRS)

Ma'aunin zinare don matakan gudu, yankunan horo da nazarin aiki

Menene CRS?

Critical Run Speed (CRS) shine matsakaicin saurin gudu da zaku iya kiyayewa a cikin yanayin daidaiton ilimin halittar jiki na tsawon lokaci (yawanci mintuna 30-60). Yana wakiltar iyaka tsakanin motsa jiki mai dorewa (aerobic) da wanda ba zai yiwu ba (anaerobic).

Ba kamar VO₂max ba, wanda ke auna yiwuwar aerobic kawai, CRS yana auna ainihin aiwatar da aiki, yana lissafin tattalin arzikin gudu da juriyar gajiya.

Me yasa CRS ke doke FTP

Duk da cewa FTP (Functional Threshold Power) shine ma'auni a cikin keke, CRS ya fi dogaro ga gudu saboda:

  • Yana kawar da buƙatar mitoci masu tsada
  • An kafa shi akan gudu/lokaci, wanda ake iya auna shi kai tsaye
  • An samo shi ta hanyar lissafi daga maki da bayanai da yawa, ba gwaji ɗaya ba
  • Yana lissafin ƙarfin aiki na anaerobic (W') mafi kyau

Samfurin Lissafi

Run Analytics yana amfani da samfurin hyperbolic gudu-lokaci (Wakayoshi et al., 1992):

t = W' / (S - CRS)

Inda:
t = lokaci zuwa gajiya
S = saurin gudu
W' = ƙarfin aiki na anaerobic (tafin makamashi)
CRS = Mahimmin Gudu

Yadda ake Lissafa CRS ɗinku

Run Analytics yana amfani da samfurin abubuwa biyu wanda ke buƙatar ƙoƙari biyu (gwajin lokaci) na tsawon lokaci daban-daban don ƙayyade gangaren layin gajiya.

Yarjejeniyar Gwajin da ake Buƙata

  1. Gwajin gajere: Mintuna 3 ko 1200m matsakaicin ƙoƙari (da farko anaerobic)
  2. Gwajin tsayi: Mintuna 10-20 ko 5000m matsakaicin ƙoƙari (da farko aerobic)

Lura: Yakamata a gudanar da gwaje-gwaje a ranaku daban-daban ko tare da cikakken farfadowa (awanni da yawa) tsakanin su. Duba Fara don cikakkun bayanai.

Bayan Lambobi: Yadda ake Amfani da CRS

CRS shine mafi ingancin tushe don saita yankunan horo. Ba kamar bugun zuciya ba, wanda ke baya kuma yana tasiri da maganin kafeyin/barci, CRS shine cikakken ma'aunin fitarwa.

Yanki Suna % na CRS Amfani na yau da kullun
1 Farfadowa < 80% Dumi-dumi, farfadowa
2 Aerobic / Jimiri 80-90% Gudu mai tsawo, ginin tushe
3 Tempo 90-95% Gudun Marathon
4 Kofa (CRS) 96-102% Tsaka-tsakin Kofa
5 VO₂max 103-110% 3k-5k taki, dogon tsaka-tsaki
6 Anaerobic 111-130% 800m-1500m taki
7 Neuromuscular > 130% Gajerun gudu, ƙarfi

Dabarun Gasa ta W'

CRS ɗinku yana gaya muku saurin da zaku iya (a ka'ida) riƙe "ba iyaka". W' (D') shine tankin ƙarin saurin da kuke da shi.

A cikin gasa, zaku iya gudu sama da CRS kawai har sai kun zubar da W'. Run Analytics na iya yin hasashen ainihin lokutan tsere dangane da yadda sauri zaku zubar da wannan tanki akan nisa daban-daban.

  • Gasa na 5k: Gudu sama da CRS, zubar da 95-100% W'
  • Rabin Marathon: Gudu dama ƙasa ko a CRS (98-100%)
  • Marathon: Gudu sosai ƙasa da CRS (90-94%) don adana glycogen

Tasirin Horarwa

Horarwa mai inganci yana canza ilimin halittar jikin ku ta hanyoyi biyu:

  1. Haɓaka CRS: Motar ku ta aerobic ta zama mafi ƙarfi. Kuna iya gudu da sauri kafin gajiya ta shiga. Wannan shine burin tushe da horo na kofa.
  2. Ƙara W': Tankin ku na anaerobic yana zurfafa. Kuna iya ɗaukar manyan ƙoƙari na tsawon lokaci. Wannan shine burin horo na tsaka-tsaki na VO₂max.

Ta hanyar lura da lambobin biyu, zaku iya ganin idan shirin horon ku yana aiki kamar yadda ake tsammani.
