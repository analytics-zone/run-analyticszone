FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does RunAnalytics get my running data?

RunAnalytics syncs with Apple Health to import running workouts recorded by any compatible device or app. This includes smart watches, fitness trackers, and manual entries. The app processes this data locally to calculate advanced metrics.

What is the CRS test and how do I perform it?

CRS (Critical Run Speed) is a scientific protocol using 2 maximum effort runs: 5K and 3K with 10-20 minutes rest between. The app calculates your aerobic threshold from these times and automatically adjusts all training zones. Repeat every 6-8 weeks to track progress.

Is my data uploaded to the cloud?

No. RunAnalytics processes all data locally on your iPhone. There are no external servers, no cloud accounts, no data transfers. You control exports: generate JSON, CSV, HTML, or PDF files and share them however you want.

Can I use RunAnalytics for trail running running?

Yes. RunAnalytics works with any running workout in Apple Health, including trail running. The app adapts to available metrics whether you're in a track or trail running, providing relevant analysis for each environment.
